Introduction

Live Caption detects speech on your device and automatically generates captions. All audio and captions are processed locally and never leave the device. You can now continuously translate someone speaking a different language in near real-time with this app. For example, translate a classroom lecture or a speech.

Application will recognize internal voice of the mobile phone and it will show caption on screen. Even it will translate internal voice to other languages
